TREASURE (트레저) (previously known as TREASURE 13) is a 10-member boy group formed through the survival show YG Treasure Box. The group consists of: Choi Hyunsuk, Jihoon, Yoshi, Junkyu, Yoon Jaehyuk, Asahi, Haruto, Doyoung, Park Jeongwoo, and So Junghwan. YG Entertainment announced on November 8, 2022, that Mashiho and Bang Yedam had left the group. On July 3, 2023, TREASURE signed a partnership contract with Columbia Records. They debuted on August 7, 2020, with their first single album, The First Step: Chapter One.
