Frequently Asked Questions about Arabic Calligraphy

What is Arabic Calligraphy?
Arabic calligraphy is the art of writing Arabic letters in an aesthetic way, used in religious and artistic writings.
How do I learn Arabic Calligraphy?
You can learn Arabic calligraphy through online lessons, specialized books, or by joining workshops.
What are the types of Arabic scripts?
There are many types of Arabic scripts, such as Kufi, Naskh, Diwani, and Ruq'ah, each with its own style.
What are the tools of Arabic Calligraphy?
The tools of Arabic calligraphy include special pens, inks, paper, and measuring tools to ensure writing accuracy.
