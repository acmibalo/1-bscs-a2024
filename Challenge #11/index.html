Artists

Leonardo da Vinci

Father of the Renaissance

Leonardo da Vinci (1452-1519) was an Italian polymath of the High Renaissance who is widely regarded as one of the greatest artists of all time. Known for his incredible intellect and talent across multiple disciplines, da Vinci was an accomplished painter, inventor, scientist, and engineer. His works, including the Mona Lisa and The Last Supper, continue to captivate audiences with their realism and innovative use of perspective and lighting.
