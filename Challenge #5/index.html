Section Article Aside

Section: Grouping Related Content

This element is used to group related content under a common heading. It is often part of the main structure of the page and helps organize related topics logically.

Article: Independent Content

This element represents self-contained, reusable content. This could be a blog post, a news article, or any content that can stand alone outside the context of the page.

Written by: Jane Doe

Aside: Supplemental Content

This element contains related but not essential information. It is often used for sidebars, advertisements, or additional context relevant to the main content.
