Agents

Jett

Swift and agile, Jett excels at outmaneuvering her opponents.

Phoenix

A fiery duelist with flashbangs and healing flames.

Sage

The ultimate healer, Sage protects her team with barriers and revival.

Brimstone

Delivers precise and powerful smoke and orbital strikes.

Sova

Find and eliminate enemies with recon arrows and drones.

Killjoy

A tactical genius who uses gadgets to control the battlefield.

Viper

Toxic controller. Uses poison walls, clouds, and a large ultimate zone to control spaces.

Cypher

Surveillance. Uses tripwires, cameras, and cages to gather intel and trap enemies.

Reyna

Self-sustaining. Consumes enemy souls to heal, become invulnerable, or enter a stealth mode after getting kills.

Breach

Disruption. Uses seismic blasts to stun enemies through walls, blind them, and clear areas.

Omen

Teleportation and smokes. Creates dark zones and blinds enemies.

Raze

Deception. Can teleport, create decoys, blind enemies, and become invisible while traversing the map.

Skye

Healing and scouting. Deploys animal-like trinkets for scouting, blinds, and heals teammates.

Yoru

Deception. Can teleport, create decoys, blind enemies, and become invisible while traversing the map.

Astra

Cosmic control. Uses stars to deploy smokes, stuns, and gravity wells.

KAY/O

Suppression. Throws grenades and knives that suppress enemy abilities and disrupt strategies.

Chamber

Sharpshooting. Utilizes teleport anchors, powerful weapons, and slows enemies with traps.

Neon

Speed-focused. Dashes, creates electric barriers, and uses an energy beam ultimate for sustained damage.

Fade

Fear-based. Reveals enemies and traps them with haunting abilities to limit their movement.

Harbor

Water-based. Deploys walls, bubbles, and other barriers to block vision and protect teammates.

Gekko

Utilizes creatures for blinding enemies, planting or defusing spikes, and capturing foes with his ultimate.

Deadlock

Specializes in trapping and controlling enemies with barriers, noise-detecting sensors, and an ultimate that drags enemies.​

ISO

Designed for one-on-one combat, excels in eliminating enemies with enhanced focus abilities.

Clove

Blocks vision with clouds, decays enemies, absorbs life force for buffs, and can resurrect temporarily.

Vyse

Sets traps, deploys damaging vines, blinds enemies, and jams their weapons with metal-based abilities​.

Maps

Haven

Features three bomb sites, allowing for dynamic gameplay.

Bind

Offers teleporters that create strategic rotation opportunities.

Ascent

Centered around a large open area with doors for tactical control.

Icebox

A snowy map with vertical gameplay and ziplines.

Split

Known for its high-ground areas and narrow choke points.

Fracture

A unique map with two opposite starting points for attackers.

Pearl

A submerged city-themed map with intricate lanes and vibrant underwater visuals, Pearl emphasizes tight angles and strategic site play.

Lotus

Another three-site map, Lotus incorporates features like rotating doors and a destructible wall, offering unique strategic possibilities.

Abyss

A dark, mysterious underwater map introduced in recent updates, Abyss challenges players with unique vertical elements and hidden pathways.

Breeze

A tropical map with wide open spaces and long sightlines, ideal for long-range combat and strategic mid control.

Sunset

An urban-themed map with tight corridors and medium-range sightlines, focusing on chokepoint control and tactical utility use.
