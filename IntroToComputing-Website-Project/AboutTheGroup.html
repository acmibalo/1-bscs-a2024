About the Group

TWICE is a popular K-pop girl group formed by JYP Entertainment in 2015 through the reality show "Sixteen." The group consists of nine members: Jihyo, Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu.

Group Name Meaning
The name "TWICE" signifies that the group aims to touch people's hearts twice: once through their music and once through their visuals.

The fandom name is "ONCE," meaning that if fans love them even once, TWICE will repay that love with twice the affection.

Debut and Achievements
TWICE debuted on October 20, 2015, with their first mini-album, "The Story Begins," featuring the hit single "Like OOH-AHH."

They have released multiple albums, EPs, and singles, becoming one of the best-selling K-pop girl groups of all time, with over 10 million albums sold and nearly 6 billion streams on Spotify.

Their breakthrough single "Cheer Up" won "Song of the Year" at major South Korean music award shows.

Recent Activities
In March 2023, TWICE released their mini-album "Ready to Be," which includes the pre-released track "Moonlight Sunrise."

They embarked on their fifth world tour, making history as the first female K-pop group to headline MLB and NFL stadiums in the U.S.

The group has received numerous awards, including the "Breakthrough Award" at the Billboard Women in Music awards in 2023.

Member Profiles
Jihyo: Leader and main vocalist, known for her strong vocals and leadership skills.
Nayeon: Lead vocalist and face of the group, recognized for her charm and stage presence.
Jeongyeon: Lead vocalist, known for her unique voice and versatility.
Momo: Main dancer and sub-vocalist, celebrated for her exceptional dance skills.
Sana: Sub-vocalist, known for her cute personality and strong fan connection.
Mina: Main dancer and sub-vocalist, recognized for her elegance and grace.
Dahyun: Lead rapper and sub-vocalist, known for her lively personality and rap skills.
Chaeyoung: Main rapper and sub-vocalist, recognized for her artistic talents.
Tzuyu: Lead dancer and visual, known for her stunning visuals and stage presence.

Global Impact
TWICE has a massive following, with millions of fans worldwide, and they have significantly contributed to the global popularity of K-pop.

They have broken records on platforms like YouTube and Spotify, showcasing their influence in the music industry.

TWICE continues to evolve and expand their reach, captivating audiences with their music, performances, and personalities.
