About

The UFC (Ultimate Fighting Championship) is the premier organization in Mixed Martial Arts (MMA), a combat sport that combines various techniques from different martial arts disciplines, such as boxing, wrestling, Brazilian jiu-jitsu, Muay Thai, and more.
The sport has evolved with specific rules, allowing punches, kicks, elbows, knee strikes, takedowns, submissions, and grappling techniques. There are three primary ways to win a fight: knockout (KO), submission, or decision (by judges’ scorecards if the fight lasts the full duration).
Established: In 1993 in the United States.
Competition Format: Fighters compete in a range of weight classes, from flyweight (125 lbs) to heavyweight (265 lbs). Events typically feature multiple bouts, with the most anticipated match being the main event, often a title fight between top contenders.
