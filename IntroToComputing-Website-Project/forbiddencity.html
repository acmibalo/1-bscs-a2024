Forbidden City - China

01 - 19 - 2017

Visiting the Forbidden City in Beijing was wild—like stepping into a whole other world. I didn’t expect it to be this big! Everywhere you look, there’s this ancient architecture with beautiful red walls and golden roofs. I honestly felt like I was walking through a living history book. It’s huge, so be ready to walk a lot, but every corner has something cool to see. The place is packed with details, from the intricate carvings to the massive courtyards. What really amazed me was how it still feels so royal, even though it’s super old. I loved how quiet and peaceful it was in some areas, even though it's crowded with tourists. It’s like you can almost hear the history here. I took my time just soaking it all in, and the vibe is totally unique. If you’re in Beijing, this is a must-see. It's one of those places that’s even more impressive in person!
