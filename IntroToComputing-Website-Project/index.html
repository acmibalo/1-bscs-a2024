It's TWICE

They are

TWICE is a popular South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment in 2015. Consisting of nine members—Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu—TWICE is known for their catchy music, vibrant concepts, and energetic performances. They gained massive success with hits like Cheer Up, TT, and Fancy, earning a global fanbase called ONCE. TWICE is one of the most influential and successful K-pop groups of the 2010s and continues to dominate the charts and music scene worldwide.
