About

Le Sserafim is a popular K-pop girl group known for their amazing performances and music!

Milestones

  • 2022: Debuted with their first album "Fearless".
  • 2022: Won their first music show award 8 days after their debut.
  • 2023: The track "ANTIFRAGILE" surpassed 500 million streams on Spotify, making it their first song to reach this milestone.
  • 2023: LE SSERAFIM's 2023 "Flame Rises" tour saw incredible ticket demand, with all performances in Seoul selling out in just 8 minutes. Additionally, the group sold out approximately 60,000 seats during their concerts in Japan.
  • 2024: LE SSERAFIM made history at the 2024 MTV European Music Awards by becoming the first-ever K-pop girl group to win the ‘Best Push’ award.

Did You Know?

  • They’re named after an anagram of "I'm Fearless."
  • Each member contributed ideas to their debut album.
  • The group’s fandom is called "Fearnot."
  • On July 19, 2022, it was announced that Kim Garam left the group. Thus, making LE SSERAFIM a 5-member group.
  • The group is under HYBE and Source Music.

Member Profiles

Kim Chaewon (김채원)

Kim Chaewon (김채원), is a Korean artist born on August 1, 2000. As the leader, vocalist, and dancer of her group, she represents the zodiac sign Leo. Standing at 164 cm (5'5") and weighing 42 kg (92 lbs), she embodies the ISTP personality type. Her representative symbols include a diamond, the color silver, and the emoji 🐯. With blood type B, her individuality shines through her Instagram handle @_chaechae_1.

Fun Fact: Kim Chaewon was born in Seoul, South Korea, and is a former member of IZ*ONE. She was the fourth member revealed in LE SSERAFIM and describes herself as lovely and loveable. She enjoys pineapple pizza and has an older sister.

Sakura (사쿠라)

Sakura (사쿠라), born Miyawaki Sakura on March 19, 1998, is a Japanese artist known for her role as a vocalist, rapper, and dancer. She stands at 163 cm (5’4”) and weighs 42 kg (92 lbs). With a Pisces zodiac sign and an INTP personality type, Sakura’s representative gemstone is pink diamond, and her color is pink. She is active on Instagram under the handle @39saku_chan, and her blood type is A. Her Korean name is Kim Yoonah (김윤아).

Fun Fact: Sakura was born in Kagoshima City, Japan, and has a younger brother. She was the first member revealed and shared that her Korean name is Kim Yoonah (김윤아). A former IZ*ONE member, Sakura enjoys lying around and admits she's not skilled at sports.

Huh Yunjin (허윤진)

Huh Yunjin (허윤진), is a Korean-American artist born on October 8th, 2001. As a vocalist and rapper, she embodies the Libra zodiac sign. Standing at 172 cm (5’7.5″) and weighing 53 kg (116 lbs), Yunjin has an INFJ personality. Her representative gemstone is emerald, and her color is green, symbolized by the emoji 🐍. With blood type B, she connects with fans through her Instagram handle @jenaissante.

Fun Fact: Huh Yunjin, born in Seoul but raised in New York, was the 6th and final member to be revealed in her group. She graduated from Hanlim Multi Arts School and trained under PLEDIS Entertainment, participating in Produce 48, where she ranked #26. Yunjin was also a former SM Entertainment trainee, where she shared a dorm with aespa’s Winter and NingNing. She’s friends with CLASS:y’s Hyungseo and aespa’s Winter and NingNing. Despite her English name being Jennifer, she prefers to be called Jen.

Kazuha (카즈하)

Kazuha, born Nakamura Kazuha on August 9, 2003, is a Japanese artist and a member of her group, serving as a sub-vocalist, rapper, and dancer. With the zodiac sign Leo, she stands at 170 cm (5'7"). Her MBTI personality is INFP, and her representative gemstone is sapphire, with blue as her signature color. She has blood type O and her Instagram handle is @zuhazana.

Fun Fact: Nakamura Kazuha was born in Kochi, Japan, and lived in Osaka from the age of 2 to 16. She also spent time in Amsterdam from 2020 to 2021, where she took ballet classes. Known for her nicknames Hachan, Zuha, Moomin, and Gazuha, she was the 5th member revealed. As an only child, Kazuha has unique personal experiences that contribute to her growth as an artist.

Hong Eunchae (홍은채)

Hong Eunchae (홍은채), is a Korean artist born on November 10, 2006. As the maknae, vocalist, and lead dancer of her group, she embodies the Scorpio zodiac. Standing at 169 cm (5'6.5"), she has an ISFJ personality. Her representative gemstone is ruby, her color is red, and her emoji is a cat 🐈. With blood type A, you can follow her on Instagram @hhh.e_c.v.

Fun Fact: Eunchae, born in Miryang, South Korea, was the third member revealed in her group. Known by the nicknames "Smile Potato" and "Pokémon," she studied at Def Music Academy. Her smile is one of her standout charms, and she has an older brother. Eunchae also has the habit of crossing her legs.
