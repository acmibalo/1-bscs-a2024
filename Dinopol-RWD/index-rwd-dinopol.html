London

London is the city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

Standing on the River Thames, London has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium.

Paris

Paris is the capital of France. It is the most populous city in the country, with a metropolitan area home to over 11 million residents.

Nestled along the Seine River, Paris has been a cultural and artistic hub for centuries, its origins dating back to its establishment by the Parisii tribe in the 3rd century BC.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan. It is the most populous city in the country, with a metropolitan area exceeding 37 million people.

Originally a small fishing village named Edo, Tokyo became a bustling metropolis in the 17th century when it became the political center of Japan.
