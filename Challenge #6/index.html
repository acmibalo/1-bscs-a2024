Antoine de Saint-Exupéry: Pilot, Poet, Dreamer

(June 29, 1900–July 31, 1944)

A French aviator and writer whose works reflect the unique perspective of a pilot and warrior who viewed adventure and danger with poetic insight. His fable, 'The Little Prince' ('Le Petit Prince'), is now a modern classic. Published in 1943 with his own illustrations, it tells the story of a young prince who travels the universe, gaining wisdom.

What Is Essential Is Invisible to the Eye

""The Little Prince" goes beyond being a children's story; it offers a deep reflection on life, love, and human connections, with iconic quotes from the book and movie that hold timeless wisdom, touching both adults and children alike.
