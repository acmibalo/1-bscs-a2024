MAKE Baranggayan

MAKE was formerly known as the Matalam Advocates for Kabataan Empowerment before becoming the Municipal Advocates for Kabataan Empowerment. It was initiated by the Matalam Vice Mayor, Ralph Ryan Rafael. The program aimed to actively involve youth in community engagement and foster young Matalameño's development into proactive leaders.

Upon its success, Congresswoman Ma. Alana Samantha Taliño Santos and Vice Mayor Ralph Ryan Rafael co-founded the Municipal Advocates for Kabataan Empowerment (MAKE) to further sustain the program. It then became a district-wide initiative across the 3rd District of Cotabato, conducted in six municipalities: Banisilan, Carmen, Kabacan, Matalam, M'lang, and Tulunan.

In collaboration with Siway University and the Cotabato Young Leaders Congress (CYLC) Alumni, the first MAKE Baranggayan was held in 2022.

MAKE Baranggayan was conducted throughout every barangay in the 3rd District of Cotabato.

MAKE 2.0

The success of MAKE Baranggayan led to the next evolution of the program, MAKE 2.0, with the theme "Honing Young Social Reformers for a Sustainable Future." This phase focuses on educating and empowering youth to address social issues and build resilient communities.

MAKE 2.0 concluded in Banisilan in December 2023.

MAKE 2.0 CARMEN 2.0 BATCH BIMALI

BIMALI represents unity among the Tri-People of Carmen, which includes Christians, Muslims, and the Indigenous Manobo-Aromanon people.

MAKE 2.0 MATALAM BATCH DIBAWBAW

Dibabawon symbolizes leadership and victory, rooted in the stories of ancient Matalam chieftains.

MAKE M'LANG and TULUNAN BATCH HIRAYA

Hiraya represents the hopes and aspirations of youth, embracing diversity and inclusivity in M'lang and Tulunan.

MAKE 2.0 KABACAN BATCH SALIGAWIS

Saligawis signifies the foundation of the MAKE-Kabacan program, representing the first batch who overcame challenges to pave the way for others.

MAKE 2.0 BANISILAN BATCH SALINDAW

Salindaw symbolizes the transmission of light, guiding the next generation with hope, empathy, and camaraderie.

MAKE 3.0

MAKE 3.0 takes leadership development to a new level by recognizing and nurturing young leaders' talents in various fields, including arts, sports, mental health, academics, and civic engagement. This phase aims to celebrate diversity, inclusivity, and leadership in shaping positive societal change.

MAKE CARMEN 3.0 BATCH DAHILAYA

DAHILAYA symbolizes resilience and leadership, inspired by the spirit of overcoming adversity.

MAKE MATALAM 3.0 BATCH DALIG

DALIG represents the growth and expansion of leadership, focusing on unity and empowerment among the youth.

MAKE BANISILAN 3.0 BATCH LIWANAW

LIWANAW embodies the light of knowledge and hope, representing the youth as leaders who guide and inspire others.

MAKE KABACAN 3.0 BATCH MAGALYABAN

MAGALYABAN is the torchbearer of leadership, committed to igniting positive change and guiding others towards progress.

MAKE TULUNAN 3.0 BATCH SIBALI

SIBALI embodies the symbolism of light and guidance, empowering youth to rise and inspire others.
