Background

Story behind MAKE

Municipal Advocates for Kabataan Empowerment is a program spearheaded by the 3rd District Representative of Cotabato, Hon. Congresswoman Ma. Alana Samantha Taliño Santos. The MAKE is a youth program under the banner of Serbisyo at Malasakit, it is conducted annually solely for the 3rd District of Cotabato. This program is designed to empower and support young individuals in their personal and social growth. It also offer mentorship, by nurturing young leaders strenght as a servant leader and developing their essential skills for community service, ultimately, unlocking their full leadership potential.
