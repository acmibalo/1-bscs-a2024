What is MAKE?

Municipal Advocates for Kabataan Empowerment (MAKE) is a program spearheaded by the 3rd District Representative of Cotabato, Hon. Congresswoman Ma. Alana Samantha Taliño Santos. MAKE is a youth program under the banner of Serbisyo at Malasakit, and it is conducted annually solely for the 3rd District of Cotabato. This program is designed to empower and support young individuals in their personal and social growth. It also offers mentorship by nurturing young leaders' strengths as servant leaders and developing their essential skills for community service, ultimately unlocking their full leadership potential.

MAKE's History

The Municipal Advocates for Kabataan Empowerment (MAKE) is currently in its 3rd year. It started in the municipalities of Tulunan, Carmen, Kabacan, Matalam, Banisilan, and will be held on December 19-20 in M'lang.
