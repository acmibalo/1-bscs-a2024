Tic-Tac-Toe Science and AI

1. Rules of Tic-Tac-Toe

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Two players take turns marking spaces in the grid, one with X and the other with O.
  • The first player to align three of their symbols in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins.
  • If all nine squares are filled without a winner, the game is a draw.
