A Story of Sacrifice and Survival

Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin) is a Japanese manga series created by Hajime Isayama. It was serialized from September 2009 to April 2021 and later adapted into a highly acclaimed anime series. The story is set in a world where humanity lives within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from man-eating giants called Titans.
