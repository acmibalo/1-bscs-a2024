Welcome to the World of Hatsune Miku

"Hatsune Miku is more than just a voicebank—she is a cultural icon and global phenomenon."

Hatsune Miku, developed by Crypton, has enchanted millions worldwide through her music, games, and fan art. This site is dedicated to exploring her journey.

Biography

Hatsune Miku was released on August 31, 2007, as the first Japanese Vocaloid developed by Crypton. Her name means "The First Sound from the Future."

Voice Provider:
Saki Fujita
Signature Color:
Turquoise
Genre:
Electronic Pop, J-Pop
Contact: info@crypton.co.jp
