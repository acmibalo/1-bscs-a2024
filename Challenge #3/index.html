About Clairo

Clairo, whose real name is Claire Cottrill, is an American singer-songwriter known for her lo-fi pop and introspective lyrics. She rose to prominence with her viral hit "Pretty Girl" and has since released critically acclaimed albums such as Immunity and Sling.

Music

Explore Clairo's discography, featuring fan-favorite tracks like "Bags," "Sofia," and "Amoeba." Her music blends indie, pop, and alternative sounds, creating a unique listening experience.
