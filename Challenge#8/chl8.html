Minecraft Cake Instructions

1. Gather Ingredients: 3 Wheat, 3 Buckets of Milk, 2 Sugar, and 1 Egg.

2. Obtain Ingredients: Harvest wheat, collect milk from cows, craft sugar from sugar cane, and gather eggs from chickens.

3. Craft the Cake: Place ingredients in this order:

  • Top row: Milk, Milk, Milk
  • Middle row: Sugar, Egg, Sugar
  • Bottom row: Wheat, Wheat, Wheat


4. Collect & Enjoy: Place the cake, then right-click to eat slices. Click to learn how
