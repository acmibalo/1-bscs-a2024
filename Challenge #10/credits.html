During our SHS, we were assigned by our ICT teacher to create a website through a Website builder. We had an idea to create a website that is a dictionary for our favorite videogames. The creation of website is based on the idea of it. Because we were a group of young gamers and we believe that a game is something that people often do for fun. In some cases, there is money to be made. For compliance, the original Gamepedia website was hosted at Gamexpedia.nicepage.io

Playing video games fulfills a purpose in our lives. This could include gaming for: relaxation, opportunities to exert control, enjoyment, creativity, socialization, prevent boredom, challenge, and achievement. It could also be used as a coping method or stress management.
