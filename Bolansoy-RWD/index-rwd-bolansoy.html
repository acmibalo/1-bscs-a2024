London

London is the capital city of England and the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

Standing on the River Thames, London has a history dating back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium.

Paris

Paris is known for its art, fashion, and culture. It is the capital of France and is often referred to as the City of Light.

Tokyo

Tokyo, the capital of Japan, is a bustling metropolis that blends traditional culture with cutting-edge technology.
