Helsingborg - Sweden

27 - 05 - 2016

Helsingborg is southern Sweden’s city by the sea, where wonderful beaches, attractions, shopping and nature are all just moments away. Enjoy the gardens of Sofiero and Fredriksal, climb the stairs to the top of the tower Kärnan, then dip your toes in the sea… later, explore fascinating exhibitions at Dunkers Culture Hall, indulge in the unique offerings of the local stores, and savor cuisines from around the world in Helsingborg’s vast selection of restaurants. Helsingborg is a charming coastal city in Sweden that offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and entertainment. With its rich history, you can explore numerous historical landmarks that will transport you back in time. The city is also known for its must-see attractions, which will leave you in awe of its beauty. When it comes to nightlife, Helsingborg has a vibrant scene with plenty of bars and clubs to choose from, making it an ideal destination for those looking to have a great time with friends. Additionally, the city is renowned for its wine and beer, offering you a chance to indulge in exquisite drinks. And of course, Helsingborg is a paradise for food lovers, with a wide range of restaurants serving delicious cuisine from around the world. A trip to Helsingborg in September will provide you with an unforgettable experience filled with history, fun, and culinary delights.
