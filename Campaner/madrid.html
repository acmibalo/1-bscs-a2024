Madrid - Spain

01 - 03 - 2021

Madrid was everything I hoped for and more! As a European traveler, I was blown away by how lively and vibrant the city is. The moment I stepped off the plane, I could feel the energy in the air. The streets are full of life, with people sitting at outdoor cafes, enjoying tapas, and just soaking up the sun. It’s the perfect blend of modern city vibes and old-world charm. One of the highlights was definitely the food. The tapas culture is real, and the variety is incredible. Whether it’s fresh seafood, jamón, or patatas bravas, every bite is a treat. I also spent time exploring the iconic Plaza Mayor and the beautiful Retiro Park, which felt like an oasis in the middle of the city. And don’t even get me started on the museums—The Prado and Reina Sofia are a must-see for any art lover. Madrid really has a way of making you feel at home while offering a whole new world of experiences. I’m already planning my next trip back!
