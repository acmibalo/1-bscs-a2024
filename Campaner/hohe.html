Hohe Tauern National Park - Austria

16 - 11 - 2022

Surreal is the best way to describe my experience in Hohe Tauern National Park in Austria. The views are absolutely breathtaking—think towering snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, and crystal-clear lakes that look like something out of a dream. As someone from Europe, I’ve seen my fair share of nature, but this place takes it to a whole new level. It feels so peaceful and untouched, like you’ve stumbled into a hidden gem that most people don’t know about. Hiking through the park was an adventure in itself. The trails are well-marked, and you can go from gentle walks to challenging mountain paths, all while surrounded by the incredible beauty of the Alps. The wildlife was a treat to spot—deer, marmots, and even golden eagles soaring above. After a long day of exploring, I couldn’t get enough of the small alpine villages where you can relax and enjoy hearty Austrian food. Hohe Tauern is the perfect place to disconnect and recharge, offering a true escape into nature’s most stunning landscapes. I can’t recommend it enough for anyone looking for a peaceful, unforgettable adventure.
